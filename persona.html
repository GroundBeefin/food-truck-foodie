Persona's

1) User

Name

Liam McGee

Age

45

Profession

Liam is a food truck owner that specializes in chicken and waffles. His truck goes out 6 days week. He has a few select locations he normally visits but wants to expand .

Technology

MacBook Pro while at home , iPad Air , and iPhone 11 Pro with unlimited data with Verizon Wireless and on a 4G network.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Liam has a strong passion for cooking and loves to meet new people. He gets frustrated that he does not have a way to interact with his customers without having to use the popular social media platforms that he is not too familiar with. He puts a lot of effort into his truck and feels disappointed when he shows up to a spot and has a slow day.

Frustrations and Needs

Liam feels limited in the small network of locations that he currently visits. He wants a way to let his customers know where to find him and also a way to interact with him. He loves to hear feedback but being behind the stove most of the time does not give him the opportunity to connect with his customers the he would like to.

Goal

Liam’s main focus is on getting more customers exposed to his fantastic street food and a way for them to comment on his delicious foods.

2) User

Name

Dee Bo

Age

42

Profession

Customer service representative for PNM in downtown Albuquerque. Part time blogger.

Technology

Microsoft user, owns a HP laptop and a Samsung Android phone.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Finds enjoyment in the variety he can get from food trucks. He likes to give feedback on experiences he has from them. He hears a lot about the new trucks around town and wants to experience them for himself. He has tried to plan out his weekend to visit some trucks but never knows where they will be. He is easily irritated and very impatient. This all often ends up getting a meal at chain spot that leaves him disappointed.

Frustrations and Needs

Dee’s main frustration is that he feels out of the loop. He is tired of trying to look thru social media posts for where a truck might be that day. He needs a way to find where a truck will be and when they will be there. Due to his lack of social media, he wants something he can easily access on its own.

Goal

Dee has one primary goal, to satisfy his hunger. He wants to keep track of trucks he likes and give his feedback on them.
